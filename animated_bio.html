Benefits of Animation as an Affective Learning tool .

'

1. Emphasises development of students’ skills and understanding of creating and responding.

2. Enables students to apply Imagination & Rational Thinking.

3. Enables students to invent and explore multiple solutions to a problem.

4. Enables students to understand the value of reflection and critical judgment in creative work.

5. Facilitates positive peer interaction, including receiving and using feedback.

6. Encourages self-motivation to create and problem solve.

7. Uses artistic literacy as a natural enhancement to learning in other content areas.

8. Fosters positive attitudes toward Art & Animation.

9. Introduces career possibilities..
